~WHO WE ARE~

When we opened Kitchen & Heaven in June 2017, our vision was to create an unassuming watering hole for the locals. In a town with endless options for food joints and restaurants, we found it challenging to find a friendly restaurant with good food and non-gaming/non-smoking environment. We wanted to provide friends and neighbors alike with the original form of social networking; a fun easy going place, and a warm atmosphere. Our staunch belief in providing exceptional quality products with friendly service in a clean and welcoming setting has helped us earn the reputation among the locals as one of the best hidden gems in Las Vegas.
Grills & Spices is the culmination of giving our best to the community by providing great food, service, and atmosphere. Tucked inside one of the premier breweries in Nevada, this collaboration project allows us another opportunity to create a memorable setting for you to catch up with friends and make new ones over tasty bites and a refreshing drinks.

HEALTH TIPS


Clean your hands properly

Prepare your food correctly

Do not skip breakfast.

Base your meals on higher fibre starchy carbohydrates.

Eat lots of fruit and vegetable.

Eat more fish, including a portion of oily fish.

Cut down on saturated fat and sugar.

Eat less salt: no more than 6g a day for adults.

Get active and be a healthy weight.

Avoid harmful use of alcohol.

Don’t smoke

Have regular check-ups

Check your blood pressure regularly
